EZNEC / NEC Modeling

Date: 2026-01-13

Model the antenna element as a segmented wire with a series RLC load at the trap location.

Use a series load with L and C values and small R to represent coil loss.

Sweep frequency to verify the trap resonance and resulting current distribution.

Tune L or C to place the current minimum at the desired frequency.

Export NEC cards and compare with measured impedance if available.
