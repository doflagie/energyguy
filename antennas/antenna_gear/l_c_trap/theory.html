L/C Trap Theory

Date: 2026-01-13

An L/C trap is a resonant in-line circuit that blocks RF current at its tuned frequency.

At resonance the trap impedance is high, so the outer part of the antenna is isolated.

Below resonance the trap is inductive, adding electrical length to the element.

Above resonance the trap is capacitive, shortening the effective element length.

Trap losses reduce efficiency, so keep coil resistance low and use quality capacitors.
