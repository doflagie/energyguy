Calculating Matching Methods

Date: 2026-01-12

  • Quarter-wave verticals can match 50 ohm coax with a simple L-network.
  • Use a shunt coil or series capacitor to cancel reactance.
  • A transformer or unun can also provide impedance transformation.
  • Add a common-mode choke at the feed to reduce feed line radiation.
