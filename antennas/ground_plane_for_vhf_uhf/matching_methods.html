Matching Methods of Ground Plane for VHF UHF

Date: 2026-01-13

  • Measure or model the feed impedance at the target frequency.
  • Choose transformer or L-network values for the impedance ratio.
  • Add a current balun or choke to limit common-mode currents.
  • Tune residual reactance with a matching network or tuner.
