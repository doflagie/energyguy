EZNEC / NEC Modeling of Rhombic

Date: 2026-01-13

  • Define geometry: wire endpoints, diameter, and height above ground.
  • Use segment length <= 0.05 lambda for accuracy.
  • Set ground type and conductivity for realistic results.
  • Place source at the feed segment and sweep frequency.
  • Inspect current distribution and pattern for anomalies.
